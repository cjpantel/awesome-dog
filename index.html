Our Story

Same Corner.
Same Family.
30+ Years.

Awesomedog started right here on the corner of Carlton and Portage in 1994. What began as one family's passion for real, honest food has become one of Winnipeg's most beloved landmarks.

We've served customers through blizzards, heatwaves, Grey Cup celebrations, and everything in between. The recipes haven't changed. The family hasn't changed. The corner definitely hasn't changed.
